Install

  1. Open this page on the Android phone and tap Download Android APK.
  2. Open the downloaded file and allow installs from the browser or Files app when Android asks.
  3. Tap Install, then open Seaweed Harvest.
  4. While online, open Collection once and wait until Phone storage says Offline ready.

Keep pending records safe: do not uninstall the app or clear its storage while collections are waiting to sync. Use Download backup before extended fieldwork.

Updates

Download the newer APK from this page and install it over the existing app. Android retains local app data when the package and signing key match. Sync or back up pending records before every update.
